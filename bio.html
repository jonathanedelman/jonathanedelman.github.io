Jonathan Edelman Bio

Education and Interests

Jonathan Edelman was born February 5, 1992 in Memphis Tennessee. He attended White Station High School in Memphis before he was accepted to Tufts University in 2010. At Tufts, he studies Mathematics and Computer Science. He enjoys the theoretical side of things, and the inherent beauty he finds in the subjects. He appreciates that Tufts has given him the opportunity to take classes in many other disciplines, too, including philosophy and sociology. Jonathan was on the varsity soccer team at Tufts, and is a member of the Zeta Psi Fraternity. After college, he hopes to use the skills he has acquired to improve the community around him.

Personal Life

Jonathan enjoys being active outdoors and spending time with his friends. He often plays pickup soccer or basketball at the Tufts fitness center, and goes hiking in the wilderness. From a young age, he has been an avid sports fan. The Green Bay Packers, the Memphis Grizzles, and Memphis Tigers basketball are his favorite teams. Some of his other hobbies include playing chess and drawing.
