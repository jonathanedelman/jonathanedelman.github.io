Jonathan Edelman Bio

Education and Interests

Jonathan Edelman was born February 5, 1992 in Memphis Tennessee. He attended White Station High School in Memphis before he was accepted to Tufts University in 2010. At Tufts, he studies Mathematics and Computer Science. He enjoys the theoretical side of things; some of his favorite classes at Tufts were "Calculus 3", "Abstract Algebra", and "Intro to Computer Science." He appreciates that Tufts has given him the oppourtunity to take classes in many disciplines. He found the philosophy class, "Making of the Modern Mind," and the sociology class, "Racial/Ethnic Minorities," especially fascinating. Jonathan was on the varsity soccer team at Tufts, and is a member of the Zeta Psi Fraternity.

Personal Life

Jonathan enjoys being active outdoors and spending time with his friends. He often plays pickup soccer or basketball at the Tufts fitness center, and when possible, he goes hiking in the wilderness. He is an avid sports fan, since being indocrinated as a Green Bay Packers supporter at a young age. He also has pride in his hometown, following the Memphis Grizzles and Memphis Tigers basketball.
